### Four steps to clarity
Getting started is simple. Build your health timeline one event at a time.


- 01 Log Your Events
  - Record doctor visits, symptoms, medications, and any health-related events. Add dates, notes, and details that matter.
- 02 Connect Everything
  - Link events to doctors, facilities, and cases. Add attachments like lab results or prescriptions to keep documents organized.
- 03 See the Timeline
  - View your complete health history in a clear, chronological timeline. Color-coded categories make patterns easy to spot.
- 04 Stay Prepared
  - Quickly recall relevant history during appointments. Access doctor profiles, past visits, and documents—all from your pocket.
